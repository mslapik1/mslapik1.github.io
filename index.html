I am fascinated by how the brain intricately processes information. I studied this as a philosophy major at Swarthmore College, as a research assistant at Johns Hopkins University and as an MD/PhD student at McGovern Medical School. During this time, I have been supported by the National Institutes of Health through a National Research Service Award and a Translational and Clinical Science Predoctoral Fellowship. Ultimately, I plan to leverage computational neuroscience to better understand and treat psychiatric disorders.
