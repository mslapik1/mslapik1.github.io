McGovern Medical School, University of Texas at Houston

I am currently an MD/PhD candidate in the Medical Physican Scientist Training Program at McGovern Medical School. I am purusing my PhD in Neuroscience in Valentin Dragoi's lab, where I use computational methods to study the visual cortex. Previously, I did studied the cerebellum in Cherie Marvel's lab at Johns Hopkins University, and studied philosophy and linguistics in the Honors Program at Swarthmore College and University of Oxford. Ultimately, I am interested in pursuing a residency in psychiatry, and using computational methods to better understand and treat psychiatric disease.

In addition to my work, I play jazz saxophone in The Chirp Chirps and volunteer at Crisis Text Line. For additional information, see my CV or Google Scholar.
