I have a long-standing interest in how the brain processes information. I studied this as a philosophy major at Swarthmore College, as a research assistant at Johns Hopkins University and as an MD/PhD student at McGovern Medical School. During this time, I have been fully funded by the National Institutes of Health through a National Research Service Award and a Translational and Clinical Science Predoctoral Fellowship. Ultimately, I plan to specialize in psychiatry, and use computational neuroscience to better understand and treat psychiatric disorders.
